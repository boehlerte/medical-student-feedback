The goal of this assessment is to assess how close a medical student is to independent functioning (entrustability) for a certain observed activity.

Patient Instructions for Survey Completion

Thank you for agreeing to provide feedback for our medical students. It is rare that they get to hear directly from patients about their performance, so your input is valued and appreciated!

All responses are confidential and anonymous and they will not affect your medical care. As you will not share any identifying information for this survey and since results are not shared immediately, your feedback will remain anonymous.
